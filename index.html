Exploratory Data Analysis of CT Electric Vehicles

This dataset contains information about electric vehicles registered in the state of Connecticut from 2021 onwards. It is important to note that the most recent data is from 12/31/2024, and that the weights of regular/non-commerical vehicles are unreported, which may lead to bias in investigating Connecticut Electric Vehicle weight or age.

Exploratory Data Analysis

We peform exploratory data analysis on the following variables present in the dataset:

  • Vehicle Make
  • Registered Usage
  • Type of EV
  • Vehicle Weight Category
  • Weight
  • Year

Final Hypotheses

Based on this EDA, we propose the following hypotheses:

  • The rise in EV registrations in Connecticut prior to 2024 was as a result of falling EV prices, tax benefits in buying an EV, and scientific advancement in EV efficiency.
  • Significant falling in EV registrations in Connecticut is due to anticipating federal policy changes that have discouraged EV registration in the past year.
  • School bus weight distribution has fewer outliers because of the standardized size and limited distributors of school buses.
